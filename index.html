Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 26, 2026

Last updated: May 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how John Barton ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Rosary mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described below. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at jbjohnbarton@icloud.com.

1. Information we collect

We collect only the information needed to operate the App and provide the features you ask for.

a. Account information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Your email address (used as your login identifier and for account-related communication).
  • A password, which is stored in hashed form. We never store your password in plain text and cannot recover or read it.

b. Purchase information

When you make a purchase:

  • On iOS, purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We receive a confirmation that a purchase occurred and the product purchased, but we do not receive your credit card, Apple ID password, or other payment credentials. See Apple's privacy policy at apple.com/legal/privacy for details.
  • For purchases made outside the App Store (web or other platforms), payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. Stripe collects and processes your payment details directly under its own privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy. We receive a transaction reference and the product purchased, but we do not store your full card number or security code.

We retain a record of purchases (date, product, transaction ID) for accounting, customer support, and tax purposes.

c. Information you provide

If you contact us by email, we receive the contents of your message and your email address so we can reply.

2. Information we do NOT collect

We want to be explicit about what we do not do:

  • We do not use third-party advertising networks.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not use Apple's IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers) to identify you.
  • We do not include third-party analytics, attribution, or marketing SDKs that collect personal data.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with data brokers.
  • We do not request access to your contacts, photos, location, microphone, camera, or other sensitive device data.

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create, authenticate, and maintain your account.
  • Enable, restore, and verify your purchases.
  • Provide customer support and respond to your questions.
  • Send essential, transactional messages (for example, password resets or purchase receipts).
  • Comply with legal obligations, including tax and accounting requirements.

We do not use your information for behavioral advertising or to build profiles about you.

4. Legal basis for processing (for users in the EU/UK)

Where the GDPR or UK GDPR applies, we process your personal data on the following bases:

  • Contract — to provide the App and the features you request, including login and purchases.
  • Legitimate interests — to keep the App secure, prevent fraud, and improve reliability.
  • Legal obligation — to retain transaction records for tax and accounting law.
  • Consent — where required, for example for any optional communications.

5. How we share your information

We share information only with the limited service providers required to operate the App:

  • Apple Inc. — App Store distribution and in-app purchase processing.
  • Stripe, Inc. — payment processing for purchases made outside the App Store.
  • Our hosting provider — to store account information and purchase records on secure servers.

These providers may only use your information to perform services for us and are bound by their own privacy and security obligations.

We may also disclose information if required to do so by law, valid legal process, or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

We do not sell your personal information.

6. How we protect your information

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your account, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for data in transit and hashed password storage. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable steps to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of your data.

7. Data retention

  • Account data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your account data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it (for example, purchase and tax records may be retained for up to 7 years to comply with applicable financial regulations).
  • Purchase records are retained as required by tax and accounting laws.
  • Support emails are retained for up to 24 months and then deleted.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated personal information.
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email jbjohnbarton@icloud.com. We respond within 30 days. You can also delete your account directly from within the App.

9. Account deletion

You can delete your account at any time from within the App's settings. When you delete your account:

  • Your email, password hash, and profile information are removed from our active systems.
  • Purchase records and any data we are required to retain for legal reasons are kept for the period described in Section 7 and then deleted.

If you cannot access the App, email jbjohnbarton@icloud.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" from the email address associated with your account.

10. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at jbjohnbarton@icloud.com and we will delete it.

11. International users

The App is operated from the United States. If you are accessing the App from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or in any country where our service providers maintain facilities. By using the App, you consent to such transfer.

Where required, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or other appropriate safeguards to protect international data transfers.

12. California privacy rights

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) gives you the right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion, and to opt out of the "sale" of personal information. We do not sell your personal information. To exercise your rights, email jbjohnbarton@icloud.com.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated through the App or by email. Continued use of the App after a change means you accept the updated policy.
